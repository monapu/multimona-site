Send monacoin

To send monacoin to someone click on the ' Send' tab (you can also use the 'Trade |  Send Monacoin' menu option).

General procedure

  1. Enter the destination address (e.g 1AhN6rPdrMuKBGFDKR1k9A8SCLYaNgXhty for MultiMona donations)
  2. Enter a label (e.g. "My donation to MultiMona") to help you keep track of who you're spending your monacoin on
  3. Enter a value (e.g. 0.01) in the MONA box

At this point if you click send MultiMona will show a confirmation screen giving you the chance to back out. If you proceed then your transaction will be submitted to the Monacoin network and should be considered irreversible from this point on.

Details

The Monacoin network requires you to specify the destination address. Any of the following will work:

  • Type in the destination address directly.
  • Copy the destination address to the system clipboard and then click on the " Paste" button next to the address field to paste it in.
  • Select a previously used destination address from the list shown at the bottom of the panel.
  • Drag and drop a Monacoin QR code onto the existing QR code or "Drag QR code here" text on the right of the panel.
  • Paste in a previously copied Monacoin URI text or monacoin QR code by using the " Paste" button on the right hand side panel.

Add in the amount of monacoin you want to send in the "Amount" field. You can type an amount denominated in monacoin (MONA) in the left entry field or the amount in your fiat currency in the right entry field. MultiMona will perform a currency conversion using your choice of exchange.

You should add a label for your personal records in the "Label" field.

You always get a confirmation screen before your monacoin are actually sent. Click on the " Send" button to see this screen. Then:

  1. Check the details on this confirmation screen to make sure the address and amount are correct.

  2. Press the " Send" button to actually send the monacoin.

When the monacoin are sent, you get a message at the bottom of the confirmation screen. Also, the number of peers (other computers on the Monacoin network) that you are connected to that have seen the transaction is shown. If there was a problem in sending your monacoin an error message will be shown instead.
