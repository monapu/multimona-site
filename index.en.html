MultiMona is a lightweight client for Monacoin, ported from MultiBit and MultiDoge.
Monacoin is a cryptcurrency which was born in japanese notorious 2ch bbs :|
Currently, most active monacoin community is in AskMona, 2ch like bbs board with Monacoin tipping system.
